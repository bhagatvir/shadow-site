Acceptance letter to [Current Team Leader’s Name]

 Dear [Current Team Leader’s Name],

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to formally request your permission to leave the [Team Name] team.

My name is [Your Name], and I currently hold the position of [Your Position] within the team. As of [Date], I would like to step down from my role and transition out of the team due to [specific reason for leaving, such as personal circumstances, a new job opportunity, career growth, educational pursuits, etc.]. This decision has been made after careful consideration and reflection on my current situation and future goals.

I have greatly enjoyed my time working with the team and have learned a lot during my tenure. The experiences and skills I have gained here are invaluable, and I am grateful for the support and camaraderie I have received from both you and my fellow team members. However, I believe that stepping away at this point is the best course of action for my personal and professional development.

I am committed to ensuring a smooth transition and am willing to assist in any way possible to minimize any disruption my departure may cause. I am available to help train my replacement, complete any pending tasks, and provide any necessary documentation or knowledge transfer.

Thank you for considering my request. I appreciate your understanding and support in this matter. Please let me know if there are any formalities or additional steps I need to take to finalize this process.

Yours sincerely,

[Your Name]
